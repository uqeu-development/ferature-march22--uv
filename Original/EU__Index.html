QUALITY AND SAFETY

Our babywear is produced in factories that meet the "High Quality" and "High Technical Competency" global assessment standards set by the MRA (Multilateral Recognition Arrangement) and CNAS (China National Accreditation Service for Conformity Assessment), both attached to the ILAC (International Laboratory Accreditation Cooperation).
We ensure each factory has a completely formalin-free environment. When dyed fabric is sent to the sewing factory, it is transported in sealed packages. After the dyeing process, as well as being tested for formalin at a chemical laboratory, fabrics are also pH-tested, checked for fluorescent agents, and inspected under a high-powered microscope for any impurities.

KIND TO BABY'S SKIN

Your baby's skin is delicate and sensitive to irritation. Babies sweat a lot, but they can't tell you when they are uncomfortable. That's why the ideal fabric for babywear needs to be highly absorbent and stretchy. We developed our 100% cotton ribbed knit fabric to meet these requirements.
Carefully selected cotton is used to produce high-quality yarn with a smooth touch. Then we perform rigorous wash-testing to verify that the fabric is durable, as well as stretchy and soft to the touch.
Both the fabric and design are created with babies in mind. The edges of each garment are bound with double stitching on the outer side for increased durability. On the inner side, which comes into direct contact with the skin, we use flat seams to reduce skin irritation. Hard-wearing yarn is used on the outer side to resist fraying, while the inner lining is made from soft, stretchy yarn for a gentle touch. Care labels are attached where they won't directly touch the skin, to prevent discomfort or irritation.
Our designs even help make outfit changes quicker and easier. We use colour-coded poppers to prevent mismatches and different-coloured stitching to help you tell inside from out.
